Q. How To Clean Sofa Set?


There are some easy ways to keep your sofa set clean: Regular dusting, weekly vacuuming and spot-cleaning the stains would keep your sofa clean. You can use half n half mixture of vinegar and water will help with rigid stains.

Q. Which sofa is best for the living room?

For choosing the best sofa set for your living room, it is important to examine each and every detail of your room like size, style, colours, layout etc. to establish harmony. Suppose if you have limited space, you can go for L shaped sofa sets or if you want to classy yet luxurious look, you can go for wooden or leather sofa sets.

Q. Which sofa is best wooden, leather or fabric?

Well, it completely depends upon your preferences and budget to pick one because the wooden, leather and fabric sofas have their own pros and cons. For example, fabric sofas are not too durable as compared to leatherette sofas and wooden sofas require more maintenance and care among three of the choices.

Q. How to set sofa in the drawing room?

If you have a spacious living room, then setting up the L shape sofas or a 3+1+1 sofa set would be the most comfortable and visually aesthetic. And if you have a compact living room then a love seat or a 3-seater would fit the best with your needs.
